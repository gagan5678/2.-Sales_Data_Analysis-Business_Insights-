# 1.-Sales_Data_Analysis-Business_Insights-
Sales Data Analysis project using Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib). Includes EDA, profit margin, MoM trends, and business insights.
# 📊 Sales Data Analysis & Business Insights

## 📌 Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze sales data to identify key trends, profit drivers, and business insights that can support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains information on:

* Sales Amount
* Profit
* Category
* Region
* Customer Segment
* Order & Delivery Details

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Handling missing values and duplicates
* Univariate and bivariate analysis

### 2. Profit Margin Analysis

* Calculated profit margin to evaluate business efficiency
* Compared margins across categories and regions

### 3. Contribution Analysis

* Identified contribution of each category and region to total sales
* Highlighted top-performing segments

### 4. Month-over-Month (MoM) Growth

* Analyzed sales and profit trends over time
* Detected volatility and seasonal patterns

### 5. Correlation Analysis

* Examined relationships between discount, sales, and profit
* Visualized correlations using heatmaps

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Sales are evenly distributed across categories, indicating a diversified business model
* Electronics and Grocery are top contributors to revenue
* North and South regions perform better, while West underperforms
* Profit margins are stable (~25%) across categories and regions
* Sales and profit are strongly correlated
* Business shows high month-to-month volatility, indicating possible seasonal effects

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus on improving performance in underperforming regions (e.g., West)
* Optimize discount strategies to improve profitability
* Invest in high-performing categories for revenue growth
* Investigate months with sharp declines to reduce volatility
